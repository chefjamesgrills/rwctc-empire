The Story

Built In Baltimore.
Trained by the Best.

Fine dining and pastry across the country. Stage at Alinea. CIA degrees. Deep in chocolate craft. And still going harder.

From the Culinary Institute of America to the kitchens of Relais & Châteaux properties, I’ve spent two decades at the edge of pastry and chocolate. Now I’m channeling that into REDWOODCHRISTMASTREECLUB LLC — a multi-brand culinary company built around bold flavors, Baltimore identity, and doing things properly.
