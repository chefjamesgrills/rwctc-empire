## The White-Label Flow (the engine)
Home base = MacBook Air running the terminal 24/7 with agents.
Pipeline:
1. Agents generate memes daily, one batch per meme Instagram account.
2. Each account posts its meme of the day.
3. Each account's bio links to its own GitHub-hosted Beacons-style page.
4. That page leads to: (a) my live streams, and (b) a white-label product store.
5. The meme of the day is printable on ~250 different products (Printify POD).
